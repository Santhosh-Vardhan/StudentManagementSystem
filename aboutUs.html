About ANIS

ANIS is a web-based platform dedicated especially for the student of Anil Neerukonda Institue of Science and Technology. ANIS aims to resolve some of the student-oriented functionalities that the official website of ANITS, anits.edu.in fails to offer. Some of these include interaction with other student, a dedicated results functionality, unofficial notifications. Apart from these, there are lots of other features that can help the students.

The main aim of ANIS is to bring the students of ANITS together so we can help each other. Help us achieve this goal by contributing to our website, being respectful and helpful to others who also use this website
